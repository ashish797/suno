# 📅 STAGE 1 — SEASONAL CALENDAR

*The Indian music release calendar — when to release what for maximum impact.*

---

## 🎵 THE 12-MONTH HINDI INDIE CALENDAR

### 🕐 JANUARY
**Theme:** New Year energy, fresh starts, resolution vibes
**Emotions that hit:** Hope, reinvention, leaving the past behind
**Good song types:** Upbeat anthems, breakup-finale songs, "new me" energy
**Avoid:** Sad songs (people are in celebration mode)
**Key dates:**
- Jan 1: New Year's Day (massive streaming spike — people discovering playlists)
- Jan 14: Lohri (Punjabi energy, but Hindi crossover works)
- Jan 26: Republic Day (patriotic can work but oversaturated)

---

### 💔 FEBRUARY
**Theme:** Valentine's season
**Emotions that hit:** Love, heartbreak (both sides), longing, nostalgia
**Good song types:** Love songs (new relationships), heartbreak songs (failed love)
**Key pattern:** Love songs work from Feb 1-14. Heartbreak songs work from Feb 15-28.
**Avoid:** Non-romantic songs (everyone's脑子 is in love/heartbreak mode)
**Key dates:**
- Feb 14: Valentine's Day (peak romance day — love AND heartbreak content surges)

---

### 🌸 MARCH
**Theme:** Spring, renewal, transition
**Emotions that hit:** New beginnings, spring cleaning in relationships
**Good song types:** Fresh start anthems, hopeful heartbreak, clean break songs
**Note:** Post-Valentine's dump — people are emotionally recovering
**Avoid:** Too heavy on romance (February overload)

---

### 🎨 APRIL
**Theme:** Holi season, pre-summer freedom
**Emotions that hit:** Joy, chaos, fun, friendship, party
**Good song types:** Party songs, fun/colour energy, travel freedom, Group dynamics
**Avoid:** Heavy emotional songs (it's a celebration month)
**Key dates:**
- Mid-March to mid-April: Holi festival period (Holi-specific songs can spike)
- April: Summer start = travel/exploration energy

---

### ☀️ MAY-JUNE
**Theme:** Summer peak, travel, freedom, school/college endings
**Emotions that hit:** Freedom, adventure, party,青春 nostalgia, breakups at graduation
**Good song types:** Road trip songs, party anthems, "last day of school" energy
**Avoid:** Sad/heavy songs (weather is happy, people are out)
**Key dates:**
- May: Exam ending = student audience floods streaming platforms
- June: Summer vacations = travel-heavy consumption

---

### 🌧️ JULY-AUGUST
**Theme:** Monsoon, romance, nostalgia
**Emotions that hit:** Romance, longing, sitting-by-the-window energy, pyaar barsaat mein
**Good song types:** Rain songs, love songs, nostalgia, slow emotional
**This is the PEAK emotional music season.** Rain + heartbreak = gold.
**Key dates:**
- July: Monsoon begins (monsoon songs spike immediately)
- August 15: Independence Day (patriotic content spikes)

---

### 📚 SEPTEMBER
**Theme:** Back to reality, new academic/professional year
**Emotions that hit:** Motivation, fresh starts, getting serious, hustle
**Good song types:** Motivational, independence anthems, self-worth songs
**Avoid:** Heavy sadness (people are in "get shit done" mode)

---

### 🪔 OCTOBER
**Theme:** Festive season begins, Durga Puja, Dussehra
**Emotions that hit:** Celebration, family, heritage, Bengali/North East festival energy
**Good song types:** Celebratory, family themes, festive but not Bollywood-overdone
**Key dates:**
- Early October: Durga Puja (Bengali audience is HUGE on streaming)
- Dussehra: Late October

---

### 🪔 NOVEMBER
**Theme:** Diwali buildup, festival peak
**Emotions that hit:** Celebration, family, prosperity, new beginnings (also applicable to new year)
**Good song types:** Celebration, family, aspirational, "treating myself" energy
**Key dates:**
- Diwali: Typically November (date varies by lunar calendar) — DIWALI is the biggest streaming spike after Valentine's

---

### 🎄 DECEMBER
**Theme:** Year-end, party season, nostalgia, Christmas crossover
**Emotions that hit:** Party, nostalgia, year-in-review, year-end reflection
**Good song types:** Party anthems, nostalgia/reflection, year-end emotion
**Avoid:** Too sad (it's a celebration month overall)
**Key dates:**
- Dec 25: Christmas (not huge in India but Western influence on streaming habits)
- Dec 31: New Year's Eve (massive streaming — playlist discovery mode)

## 🎯 OPTIMAL RELEASE STRATEGY BY SEASON

### Best months to release a viral hopeful/heartbreak song:
**July, August, February** ← Emotional peak seasons

### Best months to release an upbeat party song:
**May, June, November, December** ← Holiday/celebration seasons

### Worst months to release (most competition):
- **February** (too much competition — only the BEST heartbreak songs break through)
- **October-November** (Bollywood festival releases crowd the space)

### Easiest months to break through:
- **March** (low competition, post-festival lull)
- **January** (low competition, but new year energy helps)
- **September** (post-summer, pre-festival — open field)
