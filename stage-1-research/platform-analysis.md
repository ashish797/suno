*How each platform works, what it rewards, and how to game it.*

## 🎧 SPOTIFY INDIA

**Type:** Streaming platform + Algorithmic discovery
**Audience:** 16-35, metro + tier-2 cities
**Best for:** Building catalog streams, playlist discovery

### What Works:
- **Playlist inclusion** is the biggest growth driver
- Algorithm heavily rewards: Saves, Replays, Share rate, Skip rate
- Release Radar (algorithmic) fires 48h after release IF you have followers
- Discover Weekly fires based on listener's past behavior + similar artists

### Key Targets:
| Playlist | Type | Priority |
|----------|------|----------|
| India's Indie Scene | Editorial | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Today's Top Hits India | Editorial | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Release Radar | Algorithmic (followers) | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Mood playlists (Late Night India, Heartbreak) | Editorial | ⭐⭐ |
| Radar Rising India | Algorithmic | ⭐⭐⭐ |

### Release Timing:
- **Day:** Friday (global playlist updates happen Friday morning UTC)
- **Time:** Midnight UTC (= 5:30 AM IST) OR 9 AM IST (= ~4 AM UTC)
- **Best practical:** Upload by Wednesday/Thursday for editorial, release Friday
- Pitch via Spotify for Artists minimum **7 days before release**

### What Kills Streams:
- High skip rate (first 30 seconds matter most)
- Low save rate (means listeners don't want to come back)
- Incomplete metadata (算法 can't categorize)

## 📻 JIOSAAVN

**Type:** Regional streaming giant (India-first)
**Audience:** Broader India, tier-2/3 cities, Bollywood-crossover seekers
**Best for:** Regional penetration, Bollywood-adjacent placement

### What Works:
- **Regional language content** gets boosted
- Radio-style listening (curated stations, not just search)
- Editorial curation is significant
- Jio ecosystem integration (JioTV, JioSaavn bundle) gives embedded advantage

### Key Targets:
- JioSaavn Editorial Playlists
- Bollywood crossover (if your song sounds Bollywood-adjacent, it gets recommended in Bollywood flows)

### Difference from Spotify:
- More radio/passive listening culture
- Less algorithmic, more editorial
- Regional language boost is real

### Release Timing:
- **Friday** works but Saturday also fine
- Similar to Spotify — Friday global update cycle

## 🎬 YOUTUBE SHORTS / YOUTUBE MUSIC

**Type:** Visual-first + audio secondary
**Audience:** Gen Z heavy, 16-28
**Best for:** Virality through Shorts sync, audio discoverability

### What Works:
- **Shorts sync rate** is the key metric — how many Shorts use your audio
- A single viral Short with your song = massive streaming spike
- Shorts algorithm doesn't care about follower count — purely content-driven
- YouTube Music recommendations pull from YouTube Shorts activity

### Key Mechanism:
```
Viral Short (audio sync) → Music tab appears → Streaming discovery
```

### What Makes a Song "Shorts-Worthy":
1. Clean 15-second loop-able section
2. Strong melodic hook, doesn't require lyrics to hit
3. BPM 100-130 for upbeat / 70-90 for emotional (Shorts algorithms prefer faster)
4. Visual-friendly (not necessarily music-video quality, but the audio should feel cinematic)

### Release Timing:
- YouTube Music updates are less time-sensitive
- But getting a Short out within 24h of release boosts significantly

## 📸 INSTAGRAM REELS

**Type:** Social audio sharing
**Audience:** 18-35, urban, women-skewing
**Best for:** Cultural penetration, share rate, saves

### What Works:
- **Save rate** is the most important metric (more than likes)
- Share to Stories = viral compounding
- Reels sent in DMs = highest intent sharing
- Audio appears in trending when many creators use it in a short window

### Virality Mechanism:
```
Many creators use audio in 48h window → Audio hits "trending audio" → 
Viral discovery → Streaming spikes
```

### The " quotable line" hack:
If your chorus has a line that's screenshot-worthy (advice, philosophy, emotion), it gets shared as a Reel itself. This is a massive organic amplifier.

### What Kills Reels Performance:
- Complex/-dense lyrics (people can't extract the vibe quickly)
- Long intros (first 3 seconds must grab)
- Overly regional dialect (limits national reach)

## 🍎 APPLE MUSIC INDIA

**Type:** Premium streaming
**Audience:** Higher-income, 20-40, metro-heavy
**Best for:** Curation, editorial, prestige

### What Works:
- Editorial playlists are hand-curated (real humans)
- Less algorithmic than Spotify = more relationship-based pitching
- Connect with editors directly via Apple Music for Artists

### Release Timing:
- Similar to Spotify — Friday
- Can pitch directly to Apple Music editorial

## 🎵 THE PLATFORM INTERACTION EFFECT

In 2025-2026, viral songs don't just "blow up on one platform." They cross-pollinate:

```
YouTube Shorts goes viral → 
Audio hits Instagram Reels trending → 
People search on Spotify → 
Spotify algorithmic fires → 
JioSaavn picks it up → 
YouTube Music recommendations →
```

**Implication:** A song needs to be good enough on ALL platforms, not just one. The Shorts/Reels discovery path is the most common entry point.

## 🎯 RELEASE TIMING MASTER RULE

**Universal best practice:**
- **Upload:** Tuesday-Wednesday (before release)
- **Release:** Friday
- **Time:** 5:00-7:00 PM IST (catches evening commute AND playlist updates)
- **Pre-release promo:** Post Short/Reel teaser by Wednesday
- **Editorial pitch:** 7-14 days before release
