## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Kaagaz ke phool laaun tere liye / Khat likhun tere liye / Main khuda mein maanu nahi / Par maangun dua tere liye"

**Translation:** "I'll bring paper flowers for you / I'll write letters for you / I may not believe in God / But I'll pray for you"

**Syllable count:** 7+7+8+8 (longer hook, but structured in 4 beautiful lines)
**Structure:** Devotional paradox — "I don't believe in God, but I'll pray for you"

**What makes it work:**
- The "paper flowers and letters" imagery is instantly visual and tender
- The devotional paradox is deeply Indian: love as worship even without belief
- "Shayar bana tere liye" (I'll become a poet for you) — lover as creator
- Simple but emotionally rich — works as Reels audio
- "Tere liye" repeated creates anchor and rhythm

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** Opening chorus, ~20 seconds — immediate emotional entry
- **Devotional theme:** Religious paradox + poetry + homemaking
- **Two-person address:** "Tum" (you) throughout — intimate, direct
- **Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
