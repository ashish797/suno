## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Gul, khili aur vhi reh gayi / Tere bin jee nahi paya"

**Translation:** "Flower, bloomed and stayed there / Couldn't live without you"

**Syllable count:** 9 syllables per line (medium)
**Structure:** Flower metaphor — love as a flower that bloomed and stayed

**What makes it work:**
- Unique metaphor — "gul" (flower) is the central image
- The title itself is the hook — single word, evocative
- "Khili aur vhi reh gayi" (bloomed and stayed right there) — image of a flower that opened but didn't leave
- "Tere bin jee nahi paya" (couldn't live without you) — classic sentiment but fresh through the flower lens
- Short, poetic, works as a standalone statement

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** Within first verse/chorus, ~25-30 seconds
- **Title-driven:** Song is named after the hook word
- **Metaphor:** Flower as love — consistent throughout
- **Repetition:** "Gul" repeated + "Tere bin jee nahi paya" as refrain
- **Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
- **Poetic structure:** Less conversational, more lyrical
