## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Tu kahan? Main vahi hun jahan tu hai"

**Translation:** "Where are you? I am right where you are"

**Syllable count:** 10 syllables (medium, conversational)
**Structure:** Question + answer — conversational, intimate

**What makes it work:**
- The question "tu kahan" is simple, relatable, and immediately pulls the listener in
- The answer "main vahi hun jahan tu hai" — universally relatable longing
- Conversational tone — sounds like what someone would actually text
- Works as a Reels caption because it's a universal question
- Short question-answer structure is very memorable
