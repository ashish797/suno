## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Yeh raasta mujhe le chala / Yeh hausla mujhe le chala"

**Translation:** "This path took me / This courage took me"

**Syllable count:** 9 syllables per line
**Structure:** Letting go — "the path/ambition took me somewhere"

**What makes it work:**
- "Raasta" (path/road) as metaphor for life journey — universally relatable
- "Yeh raasta mujhe le chala" — surrender to the path, not control
- "Khayalon ke pehro se dur" — away from the shadows of thoughts
- Modern lo-fi/vaporwave aesthetic
- Works as travel/freedom anthem

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** First chorus, ~30 seconds
- **Opening:** Question format in verse — pulls listener in
- **Repetition:** "Yeh raasta mujhe le chala" repeats as anchor
- **Imagery:** Night loneliness, path/journey, distance from thoughts
- **Vibe:** Late-night introspection, lo-fi, contemplative
- **Runtime:** ~3:30-4:00
