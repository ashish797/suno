## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Tu hai kahan? / Kya main akela hi is shaher mein?"

**Translation:** "Where are you? / Am I the only one in this city?"

**Syllable count:** 4+6+6 (short question-based hook)
**Structure:** Question — searching, lonely, existential

**What makes it work:**
- "Tu hai kahan?" (where are you) — 4 syllables, pure question, instantly relatable
- "Akela is shaher mein" (alone in this city) — urban loneliness, very Gen Z
- Minimalist structure — short lines, maximum emotional impact
- Works as both romantic search AND existential isolation
- The repetition of the question creates desperation

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** First chorus, ~25 seconds — fast hook landing
- **Question-driven:** "Kahan?" repeated as the core hook
- **Urban isolation:** City loneliness theme
- **Minimalist:** Short lines, lots of white space
- **Runtime:** ~3:00-3:30
