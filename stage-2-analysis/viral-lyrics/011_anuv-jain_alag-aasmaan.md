## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Alag aasmaan bhi hai toh kya? / Yeh dil na maane"

**Translation:** "Even if we're under different skies? / This heart doesn't agree"

**Syllable count:** 11 syllables (two-part hook)
**Structure:** Rhyming question — even on different paths, heart refuses to accept the distance

**What makes it work:**
- "Alag aasmaan" (different skies) — beautiful metaphor for being on different wavelengths
- The question "to kya?" (so what?) is a rhetorical defiance
- "Dil na maane" (heart doesn't agree) — the heart vs. logic tension
- Works for both: romantic distance AND life-path differences
- Very poetic, quotable

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** Second verse/chorus, ~1:00
- **Metaphor:** Sky/distance/path — sustained throughout
- **Confrontational:** "Yeh dil na maane" — heart refuses to accept reality
- **Runtime:** ~3:30-4:00
- **Poetic but accessible**
