## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Kya kasoor hai mera"

**Translation:** "What sin is it of mine"

**Syllable count:** 5 syllables (very short, punchy)
**Structure:** Defensive question — "it's not my fault I lost myself in you"

**What makes it work:**
- The phrase "kya kasoor hai mera" is the emotional core — innocent questioning
- Sensory imagery: "zulfon ki nami" (dampness of hair), "aankhon ka nasha" (intoxication of eyes)
- Creates a "blame-shifting" emotional dynamic — the other person is the sin, not me
- Short 5-syllable hook that repeats throughout
- The body part pairs (saanson/honthon, raat/subah) create beautiful rhythm

---

## STRUCTURAL PATTERN

- **Hook lands:** First chorus, within 45 seconds
- **Sensory-rich:** Every line has a body/sensory image
- **Repetition:** Chorus repeats 3x with full repetition of "Kya kasoor hai mera"
- **Rhythm:** Pairs of opposites — raat/subah, saanson/honthon, zulfon/aankhon
- **Runtime:** ~3:30-4:00
