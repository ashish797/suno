## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Tere hi hum hain / Kya yeh pyaar kam hai"

**Translation:** "We are only yours / Is this love less"

**Syllable count:** 7 syllables (medium-short)
**Structure:** Devotional statement — "I only belong to you"

**What makes it work:**
- The phrase "tere hi hum hain" is simple but powerful — absolute belonging
- "Kya yeh pyaar kam hai" (is this love less) — questioning whether the love is enough
- Works as a devotional song but also a romantic one — lover as God metaphor
- Very popular template in Indian indie — this is the 6th most streamed Prateek Kuhad song
- Clean, simple, repeats well
