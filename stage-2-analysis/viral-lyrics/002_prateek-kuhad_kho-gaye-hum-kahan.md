## HOOK ANALYSIS

**Hook line:** "Kho gaye hum kahan"

**Translation:** "Where have we lost ourselves"

**Syllable count:** 5 syllables (very short, quotable)
**Structure:** Poetic question — loss of identity in love

**What makes it work:**
- Universally relatable phrase about losing yourself in a relationship
- Short, repeatable, quotable
- Works as a standalone statement without context
- Question format invites people to complete it
