## PART C: EMOTIONAL ARC PATTERNS

### C1. Standard Emotional Arc (16/20 songs)
```
VERSE 1 → Sets scene: who, where, what happened
  ↓
PRE-CHORUS (optional) → Builds tension
  ↓
CHORUS 1 → Hook lands — core emotional statement
  ↓
VERSE 2 → Complicates or deepens the scene
  ↓
CHORUS 2 → Hook repeats — usually unchanged or slightly escalated
  ↓
BRIDGE → Perspective shift or self-revelation
  ↓
CHORUS 3 → Final hook — emotional peak
```
**Examples:** KASOOR, KGHK, TU KAHAN, GUL, ALAG AASMAAN, TERE HI HUM

### C2. Surrender Arc (2/20 songs)
```
VERSE → Question/doubt
  ↓
CHORUS → Surrender: "the path took me" / "let the night decide"
  ↓
VERSE → Acceptance
  ↓
CHORUS → Complete surrender
```
**Songs:** RAASTA, RAAT RAAZI
**Key line examples:**
- "Yeh raasta mujhe le chala" — surrender to the path
- "Hota hai jo hone de" — let what happens, happen

### C3. Escalating Intensity Arc (2/20 songs)
```
VERSE → Muted, low energy
  ↓
CHORUS → First hit of hook
  ↓
VERSE → Builds slightly
  ↓
CHORUS → Adds layers / repeat count increases
  ↓
BRIDGE → Peak revelation ("Maa, yahan sab theek hai")
  ↓
CHORUS → Full power, maximum repetition
```
**Songs:** UNSTOPPABLE, NIDARR

### C4. Minimalist Flat Arc (2/20 songs)
```
CHORUS → Opens with full hook
VERSE(s) → Short, minimal
CHORUS → Repeats with minimal variation
```
**Songs:** TINKA, HUSN
**These are almost entirely chorus-driven with minimal verse development.**

### Most Common Instruments
| Instrument | Frequency | Usage |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| Acoustic guitar | 18/20 songs | Primary harmonic foundation |
| Soft synths/pad | 12/20 songs | Texture, atmosphere |
| Subtle strings | 6/20 songs | Emotional swell moments only |
| Trap hi-hats | 3/20 songs | Dino James only |
| 808s | 3/20 songs | Dino James only |
| Piano | 4/20 songs | Bridge moments, minimal |
| Vocal reverb | 15/20 songs | Creates intimacy/distance |
| Breath sounds | 4/20 songs | Intimacy cues (KUCH DIN, SAGE) |

### What Most Viral Hindi Indie Songs DON'T Have
- ❌ Heavy drums / loud percussion
- ❌ Auto-tune (except Ritviz, slight)
- ❌ Bollywood orchestration (no tabla, no dhol, no big brass)
- ❌ Complex song structures / odd time signatures
- ❌ Multiple bridges or long instrumental sections

**The sound is defined by what's NOT there:** minimal production, clean vocals, acoustic foundation, lots of space.

## PART H: SUNO PRODUCTION PARAMETERS

### H1. Safe BPM by Mood
| Mood You Want | BPM Range | Why |
|---------------|-----------|-----|
| Late-night intimate | 70-80 | Slow enough to breathe, intimate |
| Melancholic warm | 75-85 | Standard indie acoustic |
| Slightly energetic | 85-95 | Emotional peak without being fast |
| Electronic blend | 95-110 | Modern, slightly uptempo |
| High-energy rap | 140+ | Only for UNSTOPPABLE-type songs |

### H2. Audio Influence Slider (0.3-0.5)
**Recommendation:** 0.3-0.4 for lyrics-dominant songs (keep lyrics clear)
**Exception:** 0.5 for electronic/synth-forward songs (Ritviz-type)

### H3. What to Tell Suno About Production
1. **"Minimal production"** — lo-fi, clean, lots of space
2. **"Reverb on vocals"** — intimate, distant feel
3. **"Acoustic guitar primary"** — harmonic foundation
4. **"Soft drums"** — subtle percussion, not aggressive
5. **"No Bollywood orchestration"** — no tabla, dhol, brass
6. **"Late-night mood"** — rain, city, empty room

### H4. What NOT to Tell Suno
- ❌ "Full band arrangement" — too produced
- ❌ "Upbeat happy" — kills the viral melancholic vibe
- ❌ "Fast-paced" — loses intimacy
- ❌ "Auto-tune" — kills the confessional feel
- ❌ "High energy throughout" — should build, not start at max
