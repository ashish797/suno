## 📝 HOOK FORMULA TEMPLATES

### Formula 1: [Time/Place] + [Emotion]
> "2 AM city lights / dil udaas hai"

### Formula 2: [Common phrase] + [Unexpected twist]
> "Tera saath tha / dil nahi tha"

### Formula 3: [Question] + [Silence/Unknown]
> "Tu kya sochta hai / main kya sochta hun"

### Formula 4: [Action] + [Contradiction]
> "Jaana chhod diya / wapas aana padta hai"

### Formula 5: [Memory] + [Present pain]
> "Woh pehle aata tha / ab sirf yaad aata hai"

---

## 🔄 HOOK PRINCIPLES

1. **Max 6-8 syllables per line** (Hindi flows better in short bursts)
2. **The twist lands in the second half** — first half sets up, second half surprises
3. **Use sensory details** — city, rain, window, neon, tea
4. **Avoid abstract** — "dil" beats "emotions"
5. **Test: Would someone text this to someone?** If yes, it's working.
